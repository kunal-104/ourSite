Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about SAI Portal and AFI system access

To reset your AFI ID password, follow these secure steps:

  1. Visit the login portal and click "Forgot password?"
  2. Enter your AFI ID or registered mobile number
  3. Complete the security verification (CAPTCHA)
  4. Choose reset method: SMS OTP or Aadhaar verification
  5. Follow the instructions sent to your registered contact
  6. Create a new strong password following security guidelines

Related Articles: Password Security Guidelines, Two-Factor Authentication Setup

OTP delivery issues can occur due to several reasons. Try these solutions:

  • Check if your mobile number is correctly registered in your profile
  • Ensure you have good network connectivity
  • Wait for 2-3 minutes as SMS delivery may be delayed
  • Check your SMS inbox and spam folder
  • Try requesting OTP again after 5 minutes
  • Contact your mobile service provider if issues persist

Alternative: Use Aadhaar verification if OTP continues to fail

Profile updates require verification to maintain data integrity:

  1. Log in to your SAI Portal account
  2. Navigate to Profile Management section
  3. Click "Edit Profile" and update required fields
  4. Upload supporting documents if requested
  5. Submit changes for verification
  6. Wait for approval notification (usually 24-48 hours)

Note: Some changes may require additional verification through your sports federation
