Khelsetu Platform

KhelSetu is a next-gen sports platform built to connect athletes, coaches, and selectors under one digital roof. It simplifies the selection process with real-time fitness tests like push-ups, sit-ups, shuttle runs, BMI, height & weight tracking. The app ensures fair, transparent, and data-driven evaluation for every athlete. With its smart design, athletes can monitor progress while selectors get instant verified results. KhelSetu bridges the gap between raw talent and opportunity, making sports selection faster and more accurate. It’s not just an app – it’s the future of digital sports recruitment in India.

  • Upload training or competition videos to prove your skills and shine beyond numbers.
  • Smart AI tracks your body movements with strong accuracy, ensuring fair performance analysis.
  • No need to count – push-ups, sit-ups & more are tracked automatically with precision.
  • Instant data for athletes, coaches, and selectors, all in one place.
  • Stay motivated by monitoring growth, milestones, and fitness improvements step by step.
  • Connecting raw talent with the right stage so that no hard work goes unnoticed.
